How to use the editor Click for shortcuts and controls
  • Click Select one element.
  • Shift + Click Multi-select blocks and arrows.
  • Group Bundle the current selection so members share a legend chip and can be styled together.
  • Drag Move blocks, arrows, stage borders, the center axis, or top/bottom age brackets.
  • Double-click Edit blocks, arrows, legend chips, stage labels, or page copy.
  • Arrow keys Nudge the active selection.
  • Ctrl + Scroll Zoom the full figure around the pointer.
  • Ctrl + Drag Pan the full figure inside the viewport.
  • Ctrl + Z / Ctrl + Y Undo and redo timeline changes.
  • History Open the change list and restore an earlier figure state.
  • Delete Remove the selected element.
  • Esc Close open menus and editors.
